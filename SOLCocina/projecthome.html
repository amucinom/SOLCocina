Welcome to SOL Cocina

Located in the exciting Scottsdale Quarter development in the heart of Scottsdale, SOL Scottsdale is not your ordinary Mexican restaurant. We celebrate traditional Baja cuisine with a fresh, modern interpretation, served in a comfortably casual setting. Our menu reflects the way we like to eat in Baja—simple, fresh food that’s full of flavor. We love grilling fresh meat and Pacific seafood in the open kitchen, and we make our own salsas and sauces every day with the freshest seasonal ingredients and authentic Mexican flavors. Our vibrant bar offers inventive hand-made cocktails, featuring over 60 artisan tequilas and an inviting fire element. We love the timeless beauty of the Sonoran desert, and Baja and we’re committed to keeping it that way. Whenever possible, we have chosen products and programs that we believe to be both environmentally and socially pro-active.
